Providers & Starting Rotation Phase

Starting phase sets where each provider is in their 6-week cycle when the schedule begins. The default evenly spaces all six so exactly one is on call and one is in recovery each week.

Pain Management Clinic Provider (Permanent)

One fixed provider covers clinic every Tuesday 8AM–5PM regardless of rotation phase. During their call week: dual duty (clinic + evening call). On all other weekdays they are a regular OR provider.

Schedule Start Date

Call weeks begin on Friday. The schedule always starts on a Friday. Non-Friday dates snap to the nearest upcoming Friday.
